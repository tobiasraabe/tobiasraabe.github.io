Biography

Hi, my name is Tobias. I am a data scientist and programmer living in Hamburg.

I work on topics at the intersection of economics, statistics, and computer science, like human capital or epidemiological models.

I am also interested in productivity, best practices, and automation. Therefore, I developed pytask, a workflow management system for reproducible research.
